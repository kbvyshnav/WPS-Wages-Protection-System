Employer Forms for Onboarding – Print, Sign & Upload

General Instructions – How to Complete & Upload Employer Forms
Step Action / What to Do Download / Print Form
1
 When the employer first approaches us (or we approach them):
- Print the blank onboarding forms and master charges form.
- Give them to the employer to fill and return without signatures.
2
 Fill in the Master Charges Form with the agreed charges.
3
 After receiving the filled forms: – Create the employer in the WPS Employer Master on this site.
– Add the related persons and the agreed charges.
– Generate the updated forms and get them signed and sealed by the Employer.
4
 Once you receive the signed and sealed forms:
– Scan and upload the signed documents and related reports (e.g., site visit report) inside the KYC Documents menu.
– Inform the Head Office (HO) to review and approve.
– After approval, create the employee records.
