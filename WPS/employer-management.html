Employer Management

Didn't find the employer? Onboard a new one here:

Onboard a New Employer

First, search for the employer by name or Commercial Registration Number (CRN) in the search field.

If the employer is not found, click the Onboard New Employer button.

Then, manually enter all the required company details and proceed to onboard the employer.

Updating an Existing employer

Enter the Commercial Registration Number ( CRN) or Employer Name in the search field of the company.

The system will fetch matching employers if already onboarded.

Select the employer from the dropdown list.

Click on GO , make the required changes, and save the updates.
